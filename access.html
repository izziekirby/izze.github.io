This is an Accessibility Website

Many americans with disabilities cannot easy access and navigate through various websites due to the way they're structured. This is an example of a website that would be easier for someone with disablilities to use.

THE BLIND

although many people don't associate blind people with being internet users, it's possible for them to navigate using a narrator of sorts, or a computer program that reads what they can't see on a webiste. When website creators use a lot of different tabs or links, it can be hard for them to navigate since the blind need to go over every single header and link to figure out where they need to be...
